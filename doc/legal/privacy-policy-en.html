1. Introduction

Welcome to Luma Vault (“the App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information related to your photos, videos, and device capabilities when you use the App, and how this maps to Android permissions.

The App is designed as a local private vault: imported or captured content is primarily encrypted and displayed on your device. We do not upload your original gallery media to our servers for cloud storage as part of the current product design (if this changes, we will update this Policy and obtain consent as required by law).

3. Types of information we process

  • Content you import or capture: photos/videos from the system gallery; media captured with the in-app camera (may be kept out of the public gallery per product behavior).
  • Encrypted payloads and metadata: locally stored encrypted files, thumbnails, database records needed for vault, library management, backup/restore, etc.
  • Unlock & security: secure representations of PIN/pattern (not stored in reversible plaintext). If you enable biometric unlock, templates are managed by the OS; the App cannot read raw fingerprint or face images.
  • Device/network state (non-content): e.g. connectivity signals used by bundled components (ML model delivery, job scheduling), as described under permissions below.
